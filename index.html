My favorite hobby

Drawing

My favorite hobby is drawing. I like drawing digitally and on paper. When I draw digitally, I use a drawing tablet. It connects to the computer. It is a graphic tablet, meaning if you just look at the tablet you will not know what you are drawing. You move your pen on the tablet and it shows up on the screen. When I draw on paper I like to use notebooks. I use colered pencils and not crayons, because crayons are less detailed and can get smudges everywhere.
